MISSION

MIDATA.coop aims to help citizens to establish and own national not-for-profit MIDATA cooperatives in which they securely store, manage and control access to their personal data. MIDATA cooperatives act as the fiduciaries of their members’ data. As MIDATA members, citizens can visualize and analyze their personal data. They can actively contribute to medical research and to clinical trials by providing access to sets of their personal data across cooperatives.

MIDATA allows me to gather my different health relevent data in one secure place.
I can decide to contribute to research by giving part of my data in n anonimous way.
That way, I contribute to the development of new treatment, for OUR HEALTH.

SECURE

MIDATA uses state of art data security systems and cooperates with Swisscom Cloud Services. Data are multiply encrypted and only the members hold the keys their data.



REGIONAL

The midata model allows the construction of regional cooperatives that, through a set of common rules, permit global research projects.

NOT FOR PROFIT

TOWARDS THE COMMON GOOD

Personal data are often called a new asset class. However, the economic and societal value does not lie in the dataset of an individual person but in the aggregation of many different datasets. Therefore MIDATA is a not-for-profit cooperative.
Members will always be in full controle of their personal data. Revenues will be invested into the maintenance and further development of the platform and into research and information projects from which society as a whole profits. Members will be able to decide which projects to support.
