MADE FOR CITIZEN BY CITIZEN

MIDATA is a member-owned Swiss cooperative founded on February the 4th 2015. Its goal is to provide individuals with a secure data platform to collect, store, manage, analyze and their personal health and lifestyle data.
The cooperative is driven by the idea that every individuals should be able to control who gets access to which of his personal data, for how long and for what purpose. MIDATA is about to change the society at large by unlocking the power of personal data.
