MY DATA - OUR HEALTH

OUR HEALTH

MIDATA allows me to gather my different health relevent data in one secure place.
I can decide to contribute to research by giving part of my data in n anonimous way.
That way, I contribute to the development of new treatment, for OUR HEALTH.

MISSION

MIDATA.coop aims to help citizens to establish and own national not-for-profit MIDATA cooperatives in which they securely store, manage and control access to their personal data.

MIDATA cooperatives act as the fiduciaries of their members’ data. As MIDATA members, citizens can visualize and analyze their personal data. They can actively contribute to medical research and to clinical trials by providing access to sets of their personal data across cooperatives.

VALUES

CITIZEN OWNED

Copies of personal data are stored in citizen-owned and citizen-controlled MIDATA cooperatives.



NOT FOR PROFIT

In the same way that blood banks and citizen science platforms work best when no financial incentives are offered, MIDATA cooperatives are not-for-profit. Revenues will be reinvested into services on the platform and into research projects for the benefit of society.



OPEN SOURCE

The IT infrastructure is based on open source software and the source code and interfaces are available for improvements and for the development third party services (data analysis and visualization).



TRANSPARENT

Transparency is ensured by the fact the governance principles of MIDATA cooperatives are transparent. Members are informed and can participate in decision making processes. They can also withdraw their personal data at anytime.



SECURE

Secure citizen-controlled data access across different national cooperatives will be established to permit global research projects and clinical trials.



REGIONAL

The midata model allows the construction of regional cooperatives that, through a set of common rules, permit global research projects.
