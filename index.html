MADE FOR CITIZEN

MIDATA is a member-owned Swiss cooperative founded on February the 4th 2015. It provides individuals with a secure platform to collect, store, manage and analyze their personal health and lifestyle data.
The cooperative is driven by the idea that every individual should be able to control who gets access to which of his personal data, for how long and for what purpose. MIDATA is about to change the society at large by unlocking the power of personal data.

TRANSPAENCY

Midata users always have to agree before their personal data gets used for anything. They can at any time modify their settings and even delete their data.



CONCENT

By offering researchers a simple concent process, Midata enables individuals to open their data for specific research programms and use it for their own benefits.



SECURITY

Midata contains a strong anonimisation process with multiple encryption. API access are only given to developers who work closely to the project.
