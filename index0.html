MADE BY CITIZENS

MIDATA is a member-owned Swiss cooperative founded on February the 4th 2015. It will provide individuals with a secure platform to collect, store, manage and analyze and share their personal health and lifestyle data.
The cooperative is driven by the idea that every individual should be able to control who gets access to which of his/her personal data, for how long and for what purpose. MIDATA is about to change the society at large by unlocking the power of personal data in a democratic, citizen-centered manner.

TRANSPARENCY

MIDATA members are in full control who gets access to and who as accessed their data and for what purpose. They can modify their settings at any time and even delete their data.



SECURITY

MIDATA uses state of art data security systems and cooperates with Swisscom Cloud Services. Data are multiply encrypted and only the members hold the keys their data.



RESEARCH

MIDATA enables individuals to share their data and participate in specific research programs and clinical trials for their own benefits and for that a better citizen-centered health system.

NOT FOR PROFIT

TOWARDS THE COMMON GOOD

Personal data are often called a new asset class. However, the economic and societal value does not lie in the dataset of an individual person but in the aggregation of many different datasets. Therefore MIDATA is a not-for-profit cooperative.
Members will always be in full controle of their personal data. Revenues will be invested into the maintenance and further development of the platform and into research and information projects from which society as a whole profits. Members will be able to decide which projects to support.
